NBA Betting Metrics (Quick Guide)

  • AVI (ATS Value Index): Avg spread performance (negative SPI). Positive = market undervalues team (potential value), negative = market overvalues.
  • CRI (Cover Rate Index): ATS win rate. Above 50% = covers more often than not.

Higher AVI with decent CRI suggests betting value. Teams with negative AVI may be overvalued by the market.
