NBA Betting Metrics (Quick Guide)

  • Total DIFF: Average ATS differential across all games. Shows overall spread performance.
  • Fav DIFF: Average ATS differential when team is favorite (negative spread). How they perform when favored.
  • Dog DIFF: Average ATS differential when team is underdog (positive spread). How they perform as underdog.
  • CRI (Cover Rate): Percentage of games covering the spread. Above 50% = covers more often than not.

Positive differentials indicate beating the spread. Look for teams with positive DIFFs and high CRI for betting value.
