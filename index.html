NBA Betting Metrics (Quick Guide)

  • SPI (Spread Performance Index): Avg ATS margin. Positive = team beats the spread, negative = fails to cover.
  • AVI (ATS Value Index): -SPI. Positive = market undervalues team (potential value), negative = market overvalues.
  • CRI (Cover Rate Index): ATS wins / ATS decisions. Above 50% = covers more often than not.
  • TPI (Totals Performance Index): (team_score + opp_score) - total. Positive = games tend to go OVER, negative = UNDER.
